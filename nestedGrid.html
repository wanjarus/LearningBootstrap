Nesting with higher math: If you nest a div, the effective col value of the child will be based on the parent size.

For example, if you use a parent with col-lg-12 and a child of col-lg-6, then the child will take up 1/2 (6/12) of the parent.

If you use a parent with a col-lg-6 and a child of col-lg-6, then the child will still take up 1/2 (6/12) of the parent, but the parent will be smaller (only 6 columns)
